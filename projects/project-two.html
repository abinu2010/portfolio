About the Game

UnCaged: The Wild After Party is a single-player RPG prototype built in Unreal Engine 5. The player wakes up in a foggy forest after a night out and must escape by exploring, collecting resources, avoiding danger, and fighting through hostile encounters.

The game uses a dark forest mood with fog, ambient audio, third-person traversal, basic combat, resource collection, enemy detection, and a final escape objective.

The design focuses on a compact survival loop: explore, manage risk, find useful items, fight when needed, and progress toward the exit.

My Contribution

I built the full prototype in Unreal Engine 5, including the third-person controller, camera, locomotion, sprinting, crouching, combat feedback, and enemy behaviour setup.

I also worked on the level flow, atmosphere, animation integration, NavMesh setup, enemy patrol and chase logic, UI feedback, and final presentation polish.
